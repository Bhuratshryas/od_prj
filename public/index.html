How to Use This App

To Scan things, You can place your phone on the table in front of you to scan hands-free or rotate the camera to get closer to objects.

The software will automatically help you correct the field of view of the camera with beeping audio cues, look for keywords like "Stop" to align the object.

You can customize the information you need, like object name, brand name, expiration date and long description based on Ingredient information button
